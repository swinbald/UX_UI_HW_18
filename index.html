MY NAME IS SETH

visual artist. ux.ui designer

I am a visual artist and a ux ui designer. I have a knack for detail that borders on obsession. Good UX UI design provides relief from the stress of every day life.

Throughout my career I have experience working in team settings, multitasking, meeting deadlines and problem solving even while under pressure.

The tools I prefer are paper and pencil for sketching, miro for definition and ideation and figma for wireframing and prototyping.

Other interests include: powerlifting, cooking, cats & tattoos.

SKILLS

PROTOTYPING

Using predominantly Figma, I create prototypes that provide a clear and attractive path for the user with a focus on making sure there are no dead ends and every part of the design serves a purpose.

RESPONSIVE WEB DESIGN

When approaching responsive web design, I pay special attention to margins and spacing to ensure a smooth transition between devices. It is imperative that the user is able to have the same experience regardless of what device they are using.

USER RESEARCH

I analyze user research and try to pull out similar thoughts and ideas that I can then group together in an organized manner. I use affinity diagrams and feature prioritization to guide my design decisions.
